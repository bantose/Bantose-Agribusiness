Who We Are

Rooted in Agriculture,
Built for Growth

Bantose Agribusiness & Investment is an Ethiopian agribusiness enterprise dedicated to transforming how farming is done — from the mindset of the farmer to the markets they reach.

We provide integrated agricultural solutions that empower smallholders, agripreneurs, and investors to build profitable, sustainable enterprises across Ethiopia's rich agricultural landscape.

Across Ethiopia's diverse agro-ecological zones, Bantose operates at the intersection of traditional farming wisdom and modern agribusiness practice.

What We Offer

End-to-End Agribusiness Services

From training aspiring agripreneurs to linking farmers with markets, Bantose covers the full agricultural value chain.

🧠

Agri-Entrepreneurship Mindset Training

We reshape how farmers and youth think about agriculture — transforming subsistence mentality into entrepreneurial vision. Interactive workshops and mentorship programmes tailored for Ethiopia's agri-sector.

📋

Agricultural Project Management Training

Practical skills in planning, executing, and monitoring agricultural projects. Participants learn budgeting, resource allocation, risk management, and stakeholder engagement for farm-based enterprises.

🌾

Agricultural Production Input Supply

Reliable supply of quality seeds, fertilisers, crop protection products, and farm tools. We work with certified suppliers to ensure farmers access genuine inputs at fair, competitive prices.

🔗

Market Linkage (Local & Export)

Connecting Ethiopian farmers and agriprocessors to local retailers, institutional buyers, and international export markets. We facilitate contracts, logistics, and quality certification support.

💼

Agricultural Investment Consultancy

Expert advisory for individuals and institutions looking to invest in Ethiopia's agricultural sector. We provide feasibility studies, business planning, site selection, and investor matchmaking services.

🤝

Farmer & Cooperative Support

Capacity-building for farmer groups and cooperatives, including governance, financial literacy, collective bargaining strategies, and access to credit through partner microfinance institutions.

💼 Track 2 — Investor Briefing Programme

Agribusiness Investment Briefings

A structured briefing programme designed specifically for investors — individuals, institutions, and diaspora — seeking to understand and confidently enter Ethiopia's agribusiness sector.

Book a Briefing Session
🏭
Sector Overview & Profitability

A comprehensive overview of Ethiopia's key agribusiness sectors — crop farming, livestock, agro-processing, and horticulture — with profitability analysis and market size data for each.

👷
Labour Dynamics

Understand the labour landscape for each sector — availability of skilled and unskilled workers, seasonal labour patterns, wage structures, and workforce management considerations.

💰
Income Streams & Revenue Models

Detailed breakdown of primary and secondary income streams per sector — including direct sales, processing margins, export premiums, and by-product monetisation opportunities.

📅
Return on Investment (ROI) Timeline

Realistic ROI projections for different agribusiness investments — from short-cycle poultry and vegetable operations to medium-term coffee and oilseed ventures, with payback period estimates.

⚠️
Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Honest, sector-specific risk briefings covering climate and weather exposure, supply chain disruptions, regulatory changes, price volatility, and proven strategies to manage and mitigate each risk.

🎯
Who Should Attend?
  • 🇪🇹 Ethiopian diaspora investors
  • Local & international private investors
  • Development finance institutions
  • NGOs & impact investment funds

Format: Half-day and full-day briefing sessions available. Delivered in Addis Ababa or virtually. Customised sector-specific briefings available for serious investors. Contact us to schedule your session.
